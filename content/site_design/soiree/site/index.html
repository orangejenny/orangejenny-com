Soirée
An online meal preparation tool.
Soirée is an online meal preparation tool to help chefs at any experience level execute an event with multiple dishes. Soirée helps you find and aggregate recipes, shop for groceries with automatically created lists, and cook multiple recipes simultaneously. Soirée is tailored to your cooking skills and the equipment you have on hand.

A novice cook might use Soirée to plan her first dinner party, while an expert chef might use it to coordinate a large gathering with several courses. Our personalization features changes the interface based on your comfort level in the kitchen to make your experience relaxing and easy.

BID S2010, A3. Human-Computer Interaction Institute. Carnegie Mellon University
