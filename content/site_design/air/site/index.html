Project Overview

Welcome to the project website for the American Institutes for Research MHCI Capstone Project. For our capstone project for the Masters in Human-Computer Interaction (MHCI) program at Carnegie Mellon University, our team is redesigning the web presence of the American Institutes for Research (AIR), developing a prototype and a set of design recommendations. Our interdisciplinary team of five master's students is working for eight months to produce a working prototype using research and design methods learned over the course of the program.

American Institutes for Research is one of the largest behavioral and social science research organizations in the world. AIR's web presence reaches out to others in the research community, potential clients and partners, current and prospective employees, policymakers, media, and other groups.

Please take the time to explore this website, which covers our research methods and findings, our design process and our final design and recommendations.
