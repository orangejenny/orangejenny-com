Our Team

Our team is comprised of four individuals in the Master of Human-Computer Interaction program:

Brett Holcolmb

Project coordinator

Brett's undergraduate degree is in Computer Engineering and his professional experience is primarily in the support of programmers and artists at a video game company (Epic Games). At Carnegie Mellon, Brett is building on that interdisciplinary foundation to study HCI with an emphasis on design methods and practice.

Hajin Choi

Design lead

Hajin is a senior undergraduate student studying communication at Carnegie Mellon University. She is also pursuing human-computer interaction in an accelerated masters program expecting to graduate in December 2010. Before transferring to CMU, Hajin received a bachelor's degree in industrial design from KAIST in Korea.

Jenny Schweers

Technical lead

Prior to that, Jenny studied computer science at Worcester Polytechnic Institute in Worcester, MA, with minors in philosophy and computer engineering.

Matthew Morosky

User research lead

Matt completed his undergraduate degree in Information Sciences and Technology at Penn State and afterward worked as an IT analyst for Bayer Corporation. He is now studying human-computer interaction, with special interest in user experience design and social computing.
